## 3. Full Description (Max 4000 characters)

Never miss a lead again! MCTB Auto-Reply is the ultimate tool for contractors, tradespeople, and busy professionals who can't always answer the phone but need to stay responsive.

MCTB (Missed Call Text Back) automatically sends a professional SMS reply to anyone who calls you when you're unavailable, letting them know when you'll be back and how to reach you in the meantime.

### 🚀 KEY FEATURES:
✓ **Automated SMS Replies:** Send custom text messages to missed callers instantly.
✓ **Smart Scheduling:** Set "Active Hours" so auto-replies only send when you're actually busy.
✓ **Contact Filtering:** Choose to only reply to people in your contacts list to avoid spamming strangers.
✓ **Industry-Specific Templates (Pro):** Choose from pre-written templates for Plumbing, Electrical, HVAC, Landscaping, and more.
✓ **Human-Like Delay:** Add a natural delay to your replies so they don't feel like a bot.
✓ **Full History:** Keep track of every auto-reply sent in a clean, organized dashboard.
✓ **Privacy-First:** All your data, call logs, and messages stay on your device. No cloud storage, no tracking.

### 🛠️ PERFECT FOR:
- Construction Workers & Contractors
- Plumbers, Electricians & HVAC Technicians
- Landscapers & Gardeners
- Real Estate Agents
- Small Business Owners
- Anyone who works with their hands!

### 🔒 WHY WE NEED PERMISSIONS:
To provide automated replies, MCTB requires specific access:
- **READ_CALL_LOG & READ_PHONE_STATE:** To detect missed calls in real-time.
- **SEND_SMS:** To deliver your customized reply to the caller.
- **READ_CONTACTS:** To filter replies for known contacts only.

**Your privacy is our priority.** We do not collect or sell your data. Everything stays on your phone.

Download MCTB Auto-Reply today and turn every missed call into a potential customer!
